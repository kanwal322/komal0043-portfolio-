About Me

Hello! I'm Komal Kumari, a passionate fashion designer with a creative eye for detail and a love for exploring new ideas in design. My journey in the fashion world began with a fascination for colors, fabrics, and the art of expressing individuality through clothing.

I specialize in garment construction, fabric selection, and creative fashion concepts that bring stories to life through style. From sketching designs to final stitching, I enjoy every step of the process that transforms imagination into wearable art.

My inspiration often comes from nature, culture, and emotions — blending traditional and modern elements to create unique designs. I aim to make every piece meaningful, not just fashionable, and believe that fashion is a reflection of personality and confidence.

I’m always eager to learn new techniques, experiment with textures, and push the boundaries of creativity to design something truly special.
